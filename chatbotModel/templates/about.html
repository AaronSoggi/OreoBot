About

“Almost two-thirds (63%) of students indicated that their well-being and mental health had worsened since the start of the autumn term 2020; this has remained stable since January 2021.”

University can be the best years of your life; however, it can also cause us to experience unexpected situations which can make us feel overwhelmed and out of place. OreoBot is an application which has been designed to help university students overcome the obstacles that they face when in this particular environment and help them to cope with both anxiety and depression.

There are many issues that students face when moving away from home such as:

  • Meeting and working with new people
  • Exams
  • Coursework deadlines
  • Managing finances
  • Home sickness
  • Balancing other commitments, such as caring responsibilities or work
  • Maintaining relationships with family and friends at home
  • Leaving home
  • finding accomodation and living with new people

    • This model will give students the support they need if experiencing any of the problems above, and encourage them to seek professional help from either the student support services or professional therapists.

    Data protection regulations (GDPR) will be applied when using this application, which will ensure that whenever a student interacts with the chatbot, the data that is exchanged during conversations will remain confidential.
